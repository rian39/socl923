Rationale：

This research is an exploration of fake news, specifically in relation to the  contemporary specificity of media today and celebrity culture. Textual analysis and visual analysis are the main methods used in our research. These two kinds of methods can be combined perfectly in most of our research activities. We can do compositional analysis for the visual materials then find related articles to complete the work. Through different methods the different sides of research questions can be seen and be discovered orderly.

We search related fake news examples and extract useful information. And we find out novel approach which is to combine methods and use some of them in others. That will help us understand them deeply. Everyone try different methods and we discuss together to summarize different opinions. That will be abroad used in the final complete research.
